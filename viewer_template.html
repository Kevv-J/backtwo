read the back two · a VGC doubles companion

What it is

backtwo is a VGC doubles companion for Pokémon Champions — built for the climb. Browse a meta cut from 1,400+ tournament teams, find an archetype by your six mons, and run Gen-9-accurate damage calcs with the field, spreads, and abilities you'll actually see across every regulation.

Built by

Kevin John — one VGC player, building the tool I wanted to use.

How to reach me

Bug reports, data corrections, or rights-holder concerns: open an issue on GitHub. I'll see it.

Data sources

Teams come from the VGCPastes community archive — none of this exists without Logan and the contributors who keep it updated. Dex and move data via PokéAPI and Pokémon Showdown. Sprites via PokéAPI's sprite repo.
